Our Services

Website Design

What can be said about websites that haven’t been said before? In this day and age everyone needs a website even if you are just an individual. Websites today can provide for the many needs of both individuals and businesses. Coolbrook can analyse your requirements and build a website that fits you in the best possible way including your budget.

Business Cards

Business cards are not only essential for any business but serve as a reminder of the experience they’ve had with you even if it’s been weeks since they last talked to you. Like a logo, business cards are a way of anchoring your business in your client’s mind so that they will be reminded of you the next time their business has any needs.

Brochures

In these days of websites and instant messaging, it might seem a bit old-fashioned to provide brochures to clients, but in some circumstances they do have their uses. Some people prefer to have an actual object in their hands in order to find the information they need. If you don’t provide them with a brochure they might go to the web afterwards and find your competition’s website!

Logos

A logo is an integral part of any business and is not to be considered a superficial thing. Logos provide instant recognition for a business and helps to solidify a client’s positive experience with your business. An effective logo will associate your business with a positive and great experience and so your clients are more likely to want to come back to you time and time again.


About Us

The creative director of Coolbrook has years of experience in information technology. He started out as a computer programmer in 1985 working with the very first personal computers. He has also worked as a network administrator, helpdesk technical analyst, computer software educator and web developer.

He has worked for the Government of Canada and private corporations, along with small businesses and non-profit charities.

Along with his extensive computer experience, he brings with him years of study and practice in the arts including filmmaking, photography and graphic design. He considers the digital evolution that has transformed graphic design to be the perfect blend for his talents and interests.
